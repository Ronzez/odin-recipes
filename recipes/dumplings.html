Dumplings

Description

Delicious dumplings with beef and love!

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams of flour
  • 200 grams of beef
  • 100 grams of eggs

Steps:

  1. Make a dough
  2. Form a dumpling
  3. Leave it in freezer for 4 hours
  4. Boil dumplings before they float
  5. Enjoy your dumplings!
